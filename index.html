About Me

Hey everyone, I’m Swarnesh - MBA Class of 2027 at UNC Kenan-Flagler Business School, passionate about blending a technologist’s precision with a strategist’s vision to explore how technology, strategy, and human-centered thinking can shape the next generation of products and experiences.

Before business school, I spent 5 years building and scaling payment platforms at JP Morgan, where I worked on systems processing over $1 trillion annually across 170+ countries. My projects spanned everything from integrating new payment networks to automating revenue streams and architecting cloud-native solutions that could handle massive transaction volumes.

I am passionate about applying that technical foundation to broader business challenges whether in fintech, consumer products, or entirely new industries, bridging the “how” of engineering with the “why” of product strategy.

Outside of work, you’ll probably find me playing fingerstyle guitar, competing in a foosball match, or discovering a new culture through travel.
